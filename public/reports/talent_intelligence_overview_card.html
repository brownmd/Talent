What the data is

Behavioral evidence of AI engagement from public GitHub activity: 5.81M monthly records across 64 months (Jan 2021 – Mar 2026). Every user was caught doing AI work, not surveyed or inferred.

Signal Quality

235K users have city-level resolution (9% of total). Most provide no location (86.5% dark), so this is a self-selected, publicly visible slice — conservative floor, not full census.

Structurally shifted

2021: Small specialist group (avg score 95–125). 2025+: Broad range from frontier researchers (score 1000+) to recent adopters on model APIs.
