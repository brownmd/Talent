## Overview

A talent intelligence platform designed to ingest, enrich, monitor, and act on candidate and market signals in real time.
Essentially a talent intelligence rss feed, a talent observability tool.

---

## The Design

### 1. Seed Ingest Layer

**Purpose**  
Capture candidate and company data without interpretation.

**Inputs**
- GitHub via PyGithub
- Job boards using custom scrapers

**Outputs**
- Unmodified records stored in SQLite (`talent.db`)

---

### 2. Monitoring and Tripwire Layer

**Purpose**  
Detect market and competitor changes as they happen.

**Inputs**
- Company domains
- Job boards
- Search results
- Public people movement signals


**Detected Signal Types**
- New role creation
- Backfills
- Team expansion
- Organizational restructuring
- Stealth hiring indicators

---

### 3. Storage and Intelligence Layer

**Purpose**  
Persist, analyze, and compound intelligence over time.

**Storage**
- SQLite (`talent.db`) with full provenance

**Analytics**
- Funnel performance
