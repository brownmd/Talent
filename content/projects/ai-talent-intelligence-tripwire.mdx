## Overview

Talent Operator OS is a local-first, modular talent intelligence platform designed to ingest, enrich, monitor, and act on candidate and market signals in real time.
Essentially a talent intelligence real time news feed, curated and customized by you, for you - a talent observability tool.

---

## Design

### 1. Seed Ingest Layer

**Purpose**  
Capture candidate and company data without interpretation.

**Inputs**
- GitHub via PyGithub
- Job boards using custom scrapers

**Processing**
- Continuous ingestion
- Timestamping at ingest time

**Outputs**
- Unmodified records stored in SQLite (`talent.db`)

---

### 2. OSINT Prefilter Layer

**Purpose**  
Identify real, reachable candidates before paid enrichment.

**Inputs**
- Raw candidate records

**Processing**
- Public identity discovery including emails, social handles, and profiles.

**Outputs**
- Scored candidate set
- Threshold gate where candidates scoring 0.6 or higher advance

---

### 3. Enrichment Layer

**Purpose**  
Add structured context to high confidence candidates.

**Inputs**
- OSINT qualified candidates

**Processing**
- Employment history enrichment
- Company firmographic enrichment
- Contact completion with fallback sources

**Outputs**
- Fully enriched candidate profiles

---

### 4. Outreach Layer

**Purpose**  
Convert enriched candidates into measurable conversations.

**Inputs**
- Enriched and verified candidate profiles

**Processing**
- Template driven, multi channel outreach
- Sequenced engagement execution

**Outputs**
- Candidate raining in from the heavens

---

### 5. Monitoring and Tripwire Layer

**Purpose**  
Detect market and competitor changes as they happen.

**Inputs**
- Company domains
- Job boards
- Search results
- Public people movement signals


**Detected Signal Types**
- New role creation
- Backfills
- Team expansion
- Organizational restructuring
- Stealth hiring indicators

---

### 8. Storage and Intelligence Layer

**Purpose**  
Persist, analyze, and compound intelligence over time.

**Storage**
- SQLite (`talent.db`) with full provenance

**Analytics**
- Funnel performance
