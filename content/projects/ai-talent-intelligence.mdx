## Overview

Talent Operator OS is a local-first, modular talent intelligence platform designed to ingest, enrich, monitor, and act on candidate and market signals in real time. The system emphasizes provenance, auditability, and signal quality over volume.

---

## System Architecture

### 1. Seed Ingest Layer
**Purpose:** Capture raw candidate and company data without interpretation.

**Inputs**
- GitHub (PyGithub)
- Job boards (custom scrapers)
- ATS exports

**Processing**
- Continuous ingestion
- Timestamping
- Raw JSON persistence

**Outputs**
- Unmodified records stored in SQLite (`talent.db`)

---

### 2. OSINT Prefilter Layer
**Purpose:** Identify real, reachable candidates before paid enrichment.

**Inputs**
- Raw candidate records

**Processing**
- Public identity discovery (emails, socials, profiles)
- Confidence scoring based on OSINT signal density

**Outputs**
- Scored candidate set
- Threshold gate (≥ 0.6 advances)

---

### 3. Enrichment Layer
**Purpose:** Add structured context to high-confidence candidates.

**Inputs**
- OSINT-qualified candidates

**Processing**
- Employment history enrichment
- Company firmographic enrichment
- Contact completion with fallback sources

**Outputs**
- Fully enriched candidate profiles

---

### 4. Outreach Layer
**Purpose:** Convert enriched candidates into measurable conversations.

**Inputs**
- Enriched, verified candidate profiles

**Processing**
- Template-driven, multi-channel outreach
- Email verification prior to send
- Sequenced engagement execution

**Outputs**
- Replies
- Engagement events
- Real-time Slack notifications

---

### 5. Monitoring & Tripwire Layer
**Purpose:** Detect meaningful market and competitor changes as they happen.

**Inputs**
- Company domains
- Job boards
- Search results
- Public people movement signals

**Processing**
- Scheduled scans and crawlers
- Differential change detection
- Keyword and pattern matching
- Signal classification and scoring

**Signal Types**
- New role creation
- Backfills
- Team expansion
- Org restructuring
- Stealth hiring indicators

**Outputs**
- Real-time Slack alerts
- Persisted historical signal log

---

### 6. Referral Graph Layer
**Purpose:** Surface warm introduction paths and backchannel opportunities.

**Inputs**
- Enriched candidate profiles
- LinkedIn CSV exports
- OSINT network signals

**Processing**
- Network overlap detection
- Relationship inference

**Outputs**
- Weekly Slack digest of warm intro paths

---

### 7. Candidate UI Layer
**Purpose:** Provide recruiter visibility without custom frontend development.

**Inputs**
- SQLite pipeline data

**Processing**
- No-code UI overlay

**Outputs**
- Spreadsheet and kanban views
- Filtering, search, inline editing
- Pipeline stage tracking

---

### 8. Storage & Intelligence Layer
**Purpose:** Persist, analyze, and compound intelligence over time.

**Storage**
- SQLite (`talent.db`) with full provenance

**Analytics**
- Funnel performance dashboards
- Response rate tracking
- Enrichment cost visibility

**Intelligence**
- Geographic visualization
- Semantic candidate matching via vector search

---

## Design Principles

- Local-first and auditable
- Raw data preserved before transformation
- Signal quality over volume
- Modular, replaceable components
- Open-source friendly by default

---

## Outcome

The system enables anticipatory recruiting by detecting when to act, not just who to contact — transforming recruiting from reactive execution into real-time intelligence.
